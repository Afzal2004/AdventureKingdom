About the Novel

A Dream So Real, It Became a Story

When a 10th-grade student boards a school bus for what seems like an ordinary trip, nothing prepares him for what lies ahead. Across continents, behind ancient gates of black stone, a kingdom awaits — and within it, a frozen king who has slept for twenty years, waiting for the one who will be chosen.

This is a story about courage, destiny, and the power of the mind. It is a story that began as a dream — and refused to stay one.

The Author

Mohammed Afzal

Mohammed Afzal is an aspiring storyteller who finds inspiration in dreams and imagination. This novel is based on a powerful dream that stayed with him for years, eventually becoming the foundation of The Adventure of an African Kingdom.

Through his writing, he brings fantasy, mystery, and emotion together to create immersive adventure stories that begin in sleep and refuse to end at waking.
